Picture perfect eternity

Every cell in our body contains our DNA. On average, about 99.9% of
the DNA between two humans is exactly the same.

It is only the remaining 0.1% that makes us unique. This crucial 0.1% is
called our Genetic Fingerprint.

We turn the genetic fingerprint into a unique piece of art, enclose it in a
time capsule and mint it into an NFT - forging immortality.

WE ARE PERPETUA

At Perpetua, we have uncovered the most exemplary DNA analysis for individuals to determine
their Genetic Signature.

Our impeccably designed algorithm translates it into a genetic signature understood
and admired by all: Colour.

A specific colour palette, unique to each individual and representative of its genetic signature,
can now be used to produce any artistic output, giving a plethora of possibilities for embedding
DNA into art.

Finally, a second algorithm encrypts and embeds an Easter Egg within the palette while
generating its unique Genesys Key.

In our labs, the art becomes decipherable, and the colours are reverse engineered into the
unique Genetic Signature of a single human being.

To ensure timelessness and authenticity, every digital asset is minted into an NFT
on the designated blockchain we mint the art upon.

Data collection

The host meets with our team of experts who using
sophisticated DNA extract analysis capabilities. This
biological sample is returned to the Perpetua lab and
analysed and computed by our experts using
sophisticated machines.

Genetic Signature
extraction

Within our labs, our team of scientists analyze the DNA, extract
the Genetic Signature, and prepare it for the conversion phase.

DNA Conversion

Perpetua's first algorithm sequences the DNA. Here the Genetic
Signature extracted from the bio-sample of DNA is converted
into colours and forms a palette.

Art asset
creation

Perpetua artists use this refined colour palette to
create the final artwork. Creating a beautiful piece
containing the genetic signature's colours. The final
portrait is then returned to our team of scientists.

Genesys key
generation

In parallel to the art asset creation phase, our
scientists are busy generating a unique
decryption key. This key (known as the
Genesis key) encrypts the DNA, which is
hen embedded into the artwork.

Golden spiral
embedding

Once the key has been generated, the encrypted Genetic
signature gets embedded into the painting. A specific
number of pixels become encoded with the information
of the encrypted DNA sequence following the
Fibonacci series.

Time capsule
installation

The final artwork containing the host’s DNA is enclosed
in a glass time capsule. Both the key and portrait are
minted as NFTs. The Genesis key is given to the host,
and the portrait is handed over to the client.
